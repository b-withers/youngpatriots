What we do

Young Patriots reinvent the paper route with entrepreneurial opportunities to develop accountability and work ethic while increasing patriotism and team pride for friends, neighbors and communities. Our app helps Young Patriots manage their business sales and billing while providing American Flag and Game Day subscriptions, installations and customer service. We believe placing an American Flag in front of every home on days of remembrance will develop a greater sense of pride and unity for our communities and our Young Patriots.

Holidays Included

Memorial Day

Memorial Day is a day of remembrance for those who have died in service of the United States of America.

Independence Day

Holiday in the United States commemorating the adoption of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 1776.

Pioneer Day

Commemorates the entry of Brigham Young and the first group of Mormon pioneers into the Salt Lake Valley on July 24, 1847

Veterans Day

Observed annually on November 11, that honors military veterans; that is, persons who served in the United States Armed Forces.

Labor Day

Honors the American labor movement and the contributions that workers have made to the strength, prosperity, laws and well-being of the country.

September 11th

A series of four coordinated terrorist attacks on the United States on the morning of Tuesday, September 11, 2001. The attacks killed 2,996 people, injured over 6,000 others
